Standardized Instructions

Easy-to-follow guidance for connecting, registering, and maintaining your equipment.

Wi-Fi Connection

  1. Open the Wi-Fi settings on your device.
  2. Select the correct network name (SSID).
  3. Enter the network password securely.
  4. Confirm connection and verify the device is online.

Registering New Equipment

  1. Open the registration portal or app.
  2. Enter the device details and serial number.
  3. Assign the device to the correct location or user.
  4. Save the registration and confirm the device is listed.

Working with IoT Systems

  1. Log in to the IoT management platform.
  2. Check the device status and system alerts.
  3. Review sensor readings and performance data.
  4. Apply updates, calibrations, or configuration changes if needed.

Replacing Sensors and Equipment

  1. Power down the device or system safely.
  2. Remove the old sensor or equipment carefully.
  3. Install the new component and secure it properly.
  4. Power the system back on and verify proper operation.
